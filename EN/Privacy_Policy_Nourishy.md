# 🛡️ Nourishy Mobile Application Privacy Policy

**Date of last update:** June 21, 2026

## 1. General Provisions and Data Controller

* **1.1.** This Privacy Policy defines the rules, purposes, legal bases, and duration of the processing of personal data of users of the Nourishy mobile application (application identifier: `com.nourishy.app`), hereinafter referred to as the **“Application”**.
* **1.2.** The controller of the personal data of the Application's users is a natural person conducting business activity and making the Application available: **Marcin Głąb**, hereinafter referred to as the **“Controller”**.
* **1.3.** In all matters related to the processing of personal data, privacy protection, and the exercise of rights granted under the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation), the user may contact the Controller via the dedicated and only official email address:
  📧 **[support@nourishy.eu](mailto:support@nourishy.eu)**
* **1.4.** The Controller declares that they process users' personal data in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 (GDPR), the Polish Act on the Protection of Personal Data, and the Act on the Provision of Electronic Services.
* **1.5. Age Restriction (Art. 8 GDPR):** The Application is intended exclusively for individuals who are at least 16 years old and possess full legal capacity. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect, process, or store personal data, including health data, of children under 16 years of age. If the Service Provider becomes aware that an account has been registered by a person under 16 years of age without verifiable consent from a legal guardian, such account along with all associated data will be immediately and permanently deleted from production systems in real time.

## 📊 2. Categories and Scope of Collected Personal Data

In order to enable the use of all the advanced features of the Application, the Controller collects and processes the following categories of personal data:

* **2.1. Registration and Authentication Data:** email address, user identifier generated by the Auth0 system.
* **2.2. Profile Data and Health Information (Special Category Data – Art. 9 GDPR):** weight, height, age, gender, level of physical activity, taste preferences, selected world cuisines, nutritional goals (e.g., weight reduction, muscle building), and primarily **food allergies, intolerances, and dietary exclusions**.
  > 🧬 **Legal Note:** This data constitutes data concerning health within the meaning of Art. 9(1) GDPR. Its processing takes place exclusively on the basis of the explicit, separate, and informed consent of the user collected during the onboarding process.
* **2.3. User Content (Photos and Files):** photos of meals, dishes, and food products uploaded by the user via the mobile device camera or gallery for the purpose of analyzing their composition by external artificial intelligence (AI).
* **2.4. Data on Application Usage and Content:** information entered within the virtual refrigerator feature (owned products), shopping lists, history of generated meal plans, preferences rated using the "swipe" method, and macronutrient consumption statistics.
* **2.5. Technical and Location Data:** IP address, mobile device model, operating system version (iOS/Android), unique device identifier (IDFA/GAID), system language, unique push notification token, and geolocation (for the purpose of synchronizing the user's market, currency, and country).
* **2.6. Transaction and Purchase Data:** Premium subscription status (e.g., trial version, monthly, annual subscription), purchase date, expiration date, and transaction identifier generated by the RevenueCat system and the mobile store.
  > 💳 **Important:** The Controller does not collect, process, or see the user's payment card details or detailed bank details – payments are handled entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play.

## ⚖️ 3. Purposes and Legal Bases of Data Processing (GDPR)

Users' personal data is processed by the Controller for the following purposes, based on the indicated provisions of the GDPR:

* **3.1. Provision of Electronic Services (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR):** Processing is necessary for the performance of a contract for the provision of services (acceptance of the Regulations). This includes account registration, session maintenance (Auth0), the operation of the virtual refrigerator function, shopping cart, barcode scanner, as well as language and market synchronization based on geolocation.
* **3.2. Processing of Health Data (Art. 9(2)(a) GDPR):** The processing of profile data concerning health, physical parameters, allergies, and dietary goals to generate personalized recipes and multi-day meal plans by artificial intelligence (AI) takes place **exclusively on the basis of the explicit, voluntary, and separate consent of the user**, expressed by actively checking the appropriate checkbox during onboarding. Consent may be withdrawn at any time in the profile settings. *Withdrawal of consent to process this data results in the inability to provide services powered by AI, as regulated by § 5.5 of the Regulations.*
* **3.3. Sending Push and Local Notifications (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR):** Processing of device tokens to send notifications reminding users about meals, shopping, or system notifications (home screen widgets) based on the user's consent expressed in the settings of the device's operating system.
* **3.4. Legitimate Interest of the Controller (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR):** Processing of technical and analytical data to ensure the ICT security of the Application, detect errors and failures (*crash reporting*), optimize system performance, maintain anonymous statistics, as well as defend against or pursue potential legal claims (in particular related to the exclusion of medical liability).

## 🤝 4. Data Recipients and Entrustment (Including AI Modules)

To ensure the proper and stable functioning of the Application, the Controller uses the services of specialized external providers. Users' data is entrusted on the basis of strict data processing agreements (DPAs) or shared with the following entities:

* **4.1. Auth0 (Okta, Inc.):** The entity handling the secure registration, login, authentication, and user session management processes.
* **4.2. Railway (Railway Corp.):** The entity providing the secure cloud infrastructure and databases where the Application API is hosted and where users' encrypted data is stored.
* **4.3. Google LLC (Firebase / Firebase Cloud Messaging):** Analytical tools, crash reporting, UX optimization, and infrastructure used to send push notifications and support home screen widgets.
* **4.4. RevenueCat, Inc.:** The entity providing the technical infrastructure for managing Premium subscriptions, receipt validation, and verification of payment status from the Apple App Store and Google Play.
* **4.5. Apple Inc. (App Store) & Google LLC (Google Play):** Operators of mobile distribution platforms that process users' transaction and payment data in order to complete the Premium subscription purchase.
* **4.6. External Providers of Cloud-Based AI Models (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic):** In order to generate personalized recipes, analyze images of dishes from photos, and generate multi-day meal plans, user parameters (such as age, gender, weight, allergies, goals, and uploaded photos of dishes) are transmitted to external AI models.
  > 🤖 **AI Security:** Data transmitted to AI providers is **fully pseudonymized** (does not contain the user's email address, first name, or last name). Pursuant to the concluded agreements, AI model providers are absolutely prohibited from using this data to train public artificial intelligence models.

* **5.1.** Due to the use of services from global technology providers located in the United States (USA), users' personal data may be transferred to and stored outside the EEA.
* **5.2.** The Controller ensures that the transfer of data takes place in a fully legal and secure manner. The entities located in the USA whose services are used by the Controller (including Okta/Auth0, Google, RevenueCat, OpenAI) participate in the *EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework* program, which means that the level of data protection in these companies has been recognized by the European Commission as adequate and secure.
* **5.3.** In cases where the *Data Privacy Framework* program does not apply, the basis for the transfer is the Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) approved by the European Commission, supplemented by advanced technical measures (data encryption at rest and in transit).

## ⏱️ 6. Personal Data Retention Period

* **6.1.** Personal data associated with the User Account is processed throughout the period of account activity, i.e., for the duration of the contract for the provision of electronic services.
* **6.2.** In the event that the user decides to delete their account (via the option in the Profile -> Settings -> Delete account section), their personal data is permanently and irreversibly deleted in real time from the production database.
* **6.3.** After the account is deleted, the Controller reserves the right to store only a strictly limited and anonymized scope of data (e.g., RevenueCat transaction ID, login date) solely for the purpose of defense against potential legal or tax claims, for the limitation period of claims resulting from the provisions of the Polish Civil Code (maximum 6 years).
* **6.4.** Data processed on the basis of consent (e.g., health and allergy data, push notifications) is deleted immediately after the withdrawal of such consent by the user or deletion of the account.

## ⚙️ 8. Account Deletion Procedure vs Subscriptions

* **8.1.** Initiating the account deletion procedure in the application causes the automatic, immediate, and irreversible erasure of the email address, profile data (including allergies and health parameters), meal plan history, and photos from the servers and databases associated with the Application.
* **8.2.** > ⚠️ **Key Consumer Information:** Deleting your account in the Application by yourself **does not cause the automatic cancellation of an active Premium subscription** in the Apple App Store or Google Play. Due to technical and legal reasons dependent on the mobile store operators, the user has an absolute obligation to cancel the subscription independently in their device settings before deleting the account. Otherwise, charges will continue to be billed by Apple/Google.

## 🔒 9. Personal Data Security

* **9.1.** The Controller applies advanced technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk of violating the rights or freedoms of users.
* **9.2.** All communication between the mobile Application and the server infrastructure (API hosted on Railway) is fully encrypted using the modern SSL/TLS protocol (HTTPS).
* **9.3.** The authentication, registration, and login processes are isolated and fully secured by the dedicated Auth0 platform, which prevents unauthorized access to user accounts.
* **9.4.** Health data (allergies, weight, height) is subject to additional pseudonymization within the database, and access to it is restricted solely to authorized persons bound by an obligation of full confidentiality.
